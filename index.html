NOVO industrial floor

A two-bay test panel from a Primekss PrimX steel-fibre-reinforced concrete industrial floor system. The slab was instrumented with distributed fibre optic sensors during construction. Three static load tests were performed at the east, centre, and west of the panel using IBC water tanks to apply a 10 kN/m² uniformly distributed load on the slab surface. Click any fibre line to inspect its strain trace, or toggle the 2D strain heatmap to see how the floor responds under each load.
